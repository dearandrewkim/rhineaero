Delivering Capability

We provide professional project management, systems engineering and management consulting services to the Department of Defence and other government departments.

Our experienced and qualified team of consultants strive to solve complex project challenges for our clients, ensuring that value for money is achieved and project outcomes are controlled.

Solving Complex Challenges

Project Management
  • Project team leadership
  • Scope/budget/schedule control
  • Risk management
  • Contract development & management (ASDEFCON)
  • Effective communication & stakeholder engagement
System Engineering
  • Project team leadership
  • Requirements definition
  • System safety engineering
  • Verification & validation
  • Integration management
  • Reliability, Availability, Maintainability & Safety (RAMS)
Management Consulting
  • Enterprise architecture
  • Strategy development
  • Process mapping
  • Change management
  • Aligning organisational goals & success measures
